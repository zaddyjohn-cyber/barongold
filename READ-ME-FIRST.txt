BARON GOLD — WEBSITE
====================

HOW TO OPEN
-----------
Double-click "index.html" to view the site in your browser.
(For the photo/video galleries to load reliably, keep the whole "BARON GOLD"
folder together — index.html, the other .html pages, and the css/js/assets folders.)

PAGES
-----
index.html ............ Home
about.html ............ About Baron Gold
collections.html ...... Collections (gold, diamond, wedding, watches, lifestyle + galleries)
gold-bars.html ........ Gold Bars
custom-jewelry.html ... Custom Jewelry
sell-gold.html ........ Sell Your Gold
repairs.html .......... Gold Repairs
contact.html .......... Contact + inquiry form

PHOTOS & VIDEOS
---------------
All your photos and videos live in assets/images and assets/video.
Every photo and every video is used exactly once across the site (no repeats),
at full original quality. To swap a photo, replace the file in assets/images with
one of the same name.

PRICING
-------
By design there are no fixed prices. Every product button opens WhatsApp with a
"Request Current Price / Today's Rate" message, so you always quote the live rate.

EXCLUDED IMAGES
---------------
Two of the supplied images were left out on purpose because they carried other
jewelers' watermarks ("VeraLuxe Diamonds" and an Arabic shop tag), and one earlier
image was an Instagram screenshot of a competitor ("Jameel Gold"). Using them could
confuse your branding. The originals are still in the main folder if you want them.
